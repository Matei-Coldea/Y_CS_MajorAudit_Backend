To graduate, students must complete courses in both skills-based categories and disciplinary areas, following a structured progression:
	•	By Freshman Year: 1 course in two of three skills categories (QR, WR, Foreign Language).
	•	By Sophomore Year: 1 course in each disciplinary area (Hu, Sc, So) and each skills category (QR, WR, L).
	•	By Junior Year: Complete all skills requirements (2 QR, 2 WR, and Foreign Language).
	•	By Senior Year: Complete all distributional requirements:
	◦	Skills: 2 QR, 2 WR, and Foreign Language.
	◦	Disciplinary Areas: 2 Hu, 2 Sc, 2 So.
